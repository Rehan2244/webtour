Understanding Scroll Behaviors

This demo showcases three different tooltip scroll behaviors:

  • Reposition Mode: Tooltip follows the target element as you scroll
  • Hide Mode: Tooltip hides when target element goes out of viewport
  • Fixed Mode: Tooltip stays fixed on screen regardless of scroll
